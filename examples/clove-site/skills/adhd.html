Widen the search without losing the decision.

This skill separates ideation into distinct perspectives, then compares the strongest options and commits to a recommendation.

When to use this skill

Use ADHD mode for a consequential, genuinely open-ended decision about architecture, an API, a schema, product direction, naming, a creative concept, or a problem without an established cause.

How this skill works

  1. Confirm that the decision is genuinely open-ended, consequential, and suited to exploring several answers.
  2. Generate distinct ideas through separate perspectives before evaluating them.
  3. Score novelty, viability, and fit; flag attractive traps.
  4. Cluster by mechanism and deepen three strong, distinct survivors.
  5. Present the wide set, convergence, focused directions, and recommendation.

Do not use it for

  • Syntax, lookups, or routine implementation.
  • Bugs with an established root cause.
  • Requests for a quick, standard, canonical, or single answer.
  • Sequential brainstorming presented as isolated parallel work.
