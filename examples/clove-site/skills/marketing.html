Marketing Agent Skill Build demand from evidence.

Use this Agent Skill to research, define, execute, and measure marketing strategy grounded in buying situations, customer evidence, operational truth, and explicit choices.

Keep hypotheses separate from substantiated public claims.

Connect market diagnosis to proof and action.

This skill covers segmentation, positioning, messaging, offers, go-to-market, campaigns, lifecycle communication, brand, and measurement.

When to use this skill

You are defining who an offer fits, its positioning and messages, or a launch, campaign, channel plan, lifecycle program, pricing story, or measurement approach.

How this skill works

  1. Diagnose the market, alternatives, buying situations, demand signals, and gaps.
  2. Segment by meaningful needs and define a testable model of who the offer fits.
  3. Choose positioning and map every message to proof and qualification.
  4. Plan channels, campaigns, and lifecycle communication with clear guardrails.
  5. Separate delivery, attribution, incrementality, economics, and brand outcomes.

Govern claims

  • Never fabricate customers, outcomes, market size, rankings, or awards.
  • Place material qualifications where the audience will notice them.
  • Respect consent, suppression, accessibility, privacy, and cancellation.
  • Record claim wording, source, date, scope, reviewer, and expiry.
