Use them with intent

A stronger brief for every task.

Clove skills make the process explicit: establish what is true, make a small coherent change, and independently verify the result.

  1. 01

    Match the skill to the job

    Pick the smallest set that covers the user’s task. Cross-disciplinary work often needs more than one view.

  2. 02

    Read before acting

    Start with the complete skill instructions. Use the linked research only when the decision calls for it.

  3. 03

    Keep unknowns visible

    Inspect available evidence, then ask before choosing a direction that changes the outcome.

  4. 04

    Verify what matters

    Check behavior, content, accessibility, and implementation quality in proportion to the risk.
